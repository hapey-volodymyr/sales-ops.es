FAQ

Clear answers before you book.

How is this different from hiring a full‑time COO?

You get similar ownership and decision‑making, but without a €100K+ salary, equity package, or 3–6 month search. I typically work 1–3 days per week, own processes and metrics, and help you hire the internal team that will eventually replace me.

Which startups do you work with?

Seed–Series B, usually B2B SaaS, AI/ML, FinTech, or HealthTech. You have a product and funding, but no real operating system. If you are pre‑product or lifestyle‑oriented, I am probably not the right fit.

Do you work remotely or on‑site?

Most work is remote with clear cadences and async comms. For Madrid‑based clients, I am happy to run key workshops and kickoffs on‑site.

What happens in the free Ops Diagnostic?

We map funnel, tools, reporting and ops debt in 45 minutes. You receive a simple one‑pager with current maturity and top 3 moves. No commitment, no hard sell.

How quickly can we start?

Once we agree on scope, I can usually start within 1 week. Most projects show tangible change in the first 10 days — not after a big reveal deck.
