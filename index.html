FAQ

A few things founders ask before we start.

How is this different from hiring a full‑time COO?

You get similar ownership and decision‑making, but without a €100K+ salary, equity package, or 3–6 month search. I join 1–3 days per week, own processes and metrics, and help you hire the internal team that eventually replaces me.

Which startups do you work with?

Seed–Series B, usually B2B SaaS, AI/ML, FinTech, or HealthTech. You have a product and funding, but no real operating system. If you are pre‑product or lifestyle‑oriented, I am probably not the right fit.

Do you work remotely or on‑site?

Most work is remote with structured cadences and async updates. For Madrid‑based clients I am happy to run key workshops on‑site.

What happens in the free Ops Diagnostic?
